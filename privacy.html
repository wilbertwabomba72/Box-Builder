Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Box Builder handles information. Box Builder is a single-player puzzle game designed to work without requiring an account.

Information We Collect

Box Builder does not ask you to create an account and does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or other personal files.

The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device, including unlocked levels, star ratings, best times, and sound or music settings. This information is used only to provide game functionality.

Advertising and Rewarded Ads

Box Builder may show optional rewarded ads, such as a revive option after failing a level. Advertising may be provided by third-party ad partners. These partners may process device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and similar information in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable consent requirements.

If tracking permission is required on your device, the app or advertising partner may request permission through the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt before using data for tracking across apps or websites owned by other companies.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to operate the game, save progress, remember settings, provide optional rewarded ads, prevent abuse, measure ad performance, and maintain app stability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers or advertising partners only as needed to provide app functionality, ads, analytics related to ads, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Children's Privacy

Box Builder is designed as a family-friendly game. The app does not knowingly request personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so the issue can be reviewed.

Data Retention

Local progress data remains on your device until you delete the app or reset progress inside the game. Third-party advertising partners may retain data according to their own policies.

Your Choices

  • You can turn music and sound effects on or off in the game settings.
  • You can reset local game progress in the game settings.
  • You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove local app data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer using the support contact listed on the App Store product page.
